Project Highlights

Time Capsule App

Over the past few months, I've been working on creating an iOS app, Capsule. Capsule aims to digitize the physical time capsule experience in a virtual, phone app. Users can capture photos on the app, and store them in capsules where they cannot access the photos for a predetermined period of time. After the time has elapsed, users are presented with their old memories!
